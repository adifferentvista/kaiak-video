## PART 1: IMAGE GENERATION PROMPT (ChatGPT)

> I need an illustration for a blog featured image. Do not include any text whatsoever in the image — no labels, no words, no numbers, no UI elements with text.
>
> **Brand context:**
> My blog is called KAIAK. It helps international school leaders reduce admin overwhelm using AI systems. The tone is professional, warm, and practical — not corporate, not playful. Think: a trusted advisor who has been in the room.
>
> **Visual style:**
> - Background: uniform warm cream (#F5F0E8) across the entire image — no panels, no splits, no gradients, no vignettes, no dark elements anywhere
> - Illustration style: thick outlines, bold flat color fills, high contrast between elements — similar to editorial illustration in a premium business publication
> - Color palette: warm oranges, terracottas, and creams — stay close to #EA580C as the primary accent color. No teal, no blue, no cool colors unless they serve the concept directly
> - Must read clearly when shrunk to 200px wide — no fine detail, no soft gradients, no complex machinery diagrams
> - Mood: calm authority, purposeful, human — not playful, not corporate, not generic
> - No clichés: no funnels, no gears, no lightbulbs, no handshakes, no rockets, no brain icons, no machines, no diagrams
> - One strong central visual with generous whitespace — not a collage of many small elements
> - The illustration will sit on the RIGHT side of the final image — design it to work in a portrait-ish orientation, centered, with breathing room on all sides
> - The illustration must feel specific to this post — someone should be able to guess the topic from the image alone without reading any text
>
> **Transformation principle:**
> Where possible, show a before/after or problem/solution within the image itself — a chaotic state becoming ordered, complexity becoming simple, overwhelm becoming calm. This should be visible without any text labels.
>
> **What to avoid specifically:**
> - Funnel diagrams
> - Gear/cog machinery
> - Abstract tech patterns
> - Cool color palettes (teal, blue, purple)
> - Stock illustration clichés
> - Anything that could illustrate a generic productivity or AI article
>
